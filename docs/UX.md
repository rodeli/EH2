## Applications

### Public Site (`apps/public`)

**Purpose**: Marketing and lead generation

**Key Flows**:
- Homepage with value proposition
- Lead capture form
- Educational content
- Contact information

**Design Principles**:
- Clear, accessible
- Mobile-responsive
- Fast loading (edge-optimized)

### Client Portal (`apps/client`)

**Purpose**: Client self-service dashboard

**Key Flows**:
- Login/authentication
- Expediente status view
- Document upload
- Communication with team

**Design Principles**:
- Simple, intuitive
- Secure
- Mobile-friendly

### Admin Portal (`apps/admin`)

**Purpose**: Internal backoffice operations

**Key Flows**:
- Lead management
- Expediente management
- User management
- Reporting and analytics

**Design Principles**:
- Efficient workflows
- Data-dense interfaces
- Role-based access
